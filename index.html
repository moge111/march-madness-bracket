Overview

A data-driven March Madness bracket predictor that blends efficiency metrics, historical seed performance, momentum, injury adjustments, and home-court proximity into a single win probability for every matchup. Generate thousands of simulated brackets, track them on a live leaderboard, and watch which ones survive as real results come in. All data syncs across devices via Firebase.

The Prediction Model

Each matchup probability is a weighted blend of three core components, plus several modifiers. All weights are fully customizable via Customize Model.

1. Efficiency Win Probability

The backbone of the model. Uses a logistic function on the difference between two teams' injury-adjusted AdjEM. Research shows efficiency margin alone predicts ~70% of tournament outcomes.

P(win) = 1 / (1 + 10(-AdjEM_diff / 10.25))

A 10-point AdjEM advantage = ~71% win rate. A 20-point gap = ~90%. Default weight: 90/100.

2. Historical Seed Performance

Win rates from every NCAA tournament game, 1985-2025 (41 years of data):

  • 1 vs 16: 98.7% (155-2)
  • 2 vs 15: 93.6% (147-10)
  • 3 vs 14: 86.6% (136-21)
  • 4 vs 13: 79.6% (125-32)
  • 5 vs 12: 64.3% (101-56) — the classic upset spot
  • 6 vs 11: 66.2% (104-53)
  • 7 vs 10: 64.3% (101-56)
  • 8 vs 9: 54.8% (86-71) — near coin flip

Default weight: 15/100. Useful for pool strategy but less predictive than efficiency data.

3. Stat Edge Composite

A blend of 11 individual stat comparisons, each with its own customizable weight:

  • Defense (75) — AdjD comparison. Defense travels in March.
  • Turnover Rate (60) — Low-TO teams handle pressure.
  • Free Throw % (55) — Close games decided at the line.
  • Strength of Schedule (50) — Context for all other stats.
  • 3PT Shooting (45) — High variance, hot shooting wins games.
  • Defensive Rebounding (40) — Ends opponent possessions.
  • Momentum (35) — Last-10-game AdjEM trend. Hot teams outperform.
  • Tempo (30) — Slow games = more variance = more upsets.
  • Experience (25) — Veteran rosters handle tournament pressure.
  • Offensive Rebounding (25) — Second-chance points.

Model Modifiers

Upset Adjustment

Scans for red flags that historically correlate with upsets and nudges the underdog's probability up: small efficiency gap despite large seed gap, 3PT shooting edge, slow tempo, turnover-prone favorite, weak FT shooting, experienced underdog, weak favorite defense, strong underdog defense, and underdog momentum surge.

Round-by-Round Scaling

The model adjusts weights by tournament round because different factors matter at different stages:

  • R64 & R32: Default weights. Upsets most common here.
  • Sweet 16 & Elite 8: Defense +20%, Experience +25%, upset adjustment reduced 30%.
  • Final Four & Championship: Defense +30%, Experience +30%, FT% +15%, upset adjustment reduced 50%.
Injury Adjustments

Key player injuries reduce a team's effective AdjEM in predictions. Each injury has an impact level (Star -5, Key Starter -3, Rotation -1.5, Role Player -0.5) and a status modifier (Out = full penalty, Doubtful = 75%, Questionable = 40%). Injuries can have an expected return round — for example, a player out for the first weekend but expected back for the Sweet 16 will only penalize the team in early rounds. Only injuries not already reflected in the team's season stats are penalized.

Home-Region Proximity

Teams playing near their home state get a small probability boost (~1.5%) in the first two rounds only. Based on venue-to-state mappings for all 8 first/second round sites.

Key Stats

AdjEM — Adjusted Efficiency Margin

Points per 100 possessions a team outscores opponents by, adjusted for opponent strength. The single most important number. Elite: 25+, contenders: 30+, average tourney team: 12-18.

AdjO / AdjD — Offensive & Defensive Efficiency

Points scored (AdjO) and allowed (AdjD) per 100 possessions, adjusted for opponents. Higher AdjO = better offense, lower AdjD = better defense. Champions almost always have a top-25 defense (AdjD under 96).

Last 10 AdjEM — Momentum

Adjusted efficiency margin over the team's last 10 games. Captures whether a team is peaking or slumping heading into the tournament. A team with a Last 10 AdjEM much higher than their season AdjEM is surging.

Championship Title Score

A 0-100 composite measuring how well a team matches the profile of past champions. Factors in AdjEM (25 pts), defense (20 pts), FT% (15 pts), seed (15 pts), experience (15 pts), and turnover rate (10 pts). Continuous scoring with no cliff effects. 70+ = legit contender, 45-69 = Final Four potential, below 45 = deep run unlikely.

Bracket Features

Vegas Spreads & Game Times

Every Round of 64 matchup shows the Vegas point spread and the game date, tip time (MST), and TV channel directly on the bracket card.

Auto-Fill

Auto-Fill Favorites picks the higher-probability team in every game. Auto-Fill w/ Upsets uses weighted coin flips so some underdogs win, creating a more realistic bracket. Both respect all model weights, injuries, and round adjustments.

Customize Model

Adjust any of the 12 stat weights from 0-100 using sliders. Changes immediately affect all predictions, auto-fill results, and newly generated sim brackets. Reset to defaults anytime.

Leaderboard & Sim Brackets

Sim Brackets

Generate thousands of simulated brackets using probability-weighted coin flips from the model. Each sim is unique. Use them to see which teams the model likes most (championship win rates, Final Four rates, Elite 8 rates), and to compete against on the leaderboard. Sims are stored locally on your device.

Scoring

ESPN standard scoring: R64 = 1 pt, R32 = 2, Sweet 16 = 4, Elite 8 = 8, Final Four = 16, Championship = 32. Max possible = 192. Scoring only runs once game results are entered.

Perfect Bracket Tracking

Two columns track bracket survival: Perfect? (Yes/No) and Busted In (the round of the first wrong pick). Watch how many of your sims stay alive as results come in.

Search & Filter

Search brackets by name, champion, or any team in the picks. Use the champion dropdown to filter to brackets where a specific team wins it all. Click View on any bracket to load it into the bracket tab without losing your own picks.

Sim Stats

Automatically calculated from all your sims: championship win rates, Final Four appearance rates, Elite 8 rates, unique bracket count, unique Final Four combinations, and number of different champions. Bar charts with hover highlights.

Cloud Sync

Human-submitted brackets and game results sync across all devices via Firebase. Submit a bracket on your phone, see it on your desktop. Enter game results on any device, scores update everywhere in real-time. Sim brackets stay local to each device. Look for "Cloud sync active" on the bracket tab to confirm the connection.

Using the Tabs

Bracket

Click a team to pick them. They advance automatically. Change an earlier pick and later rounds clear. Shows Vegas spreads, game times (MST), and TV channels for Round of 64. Your picks save automatically.

Matchup Analyzer

Head-to-head comparison of any two teams. Shows predicted winner, win probability, stat-by-stat bars, upset factors, and championship viability scores. Click Analyze under any bracket matchup to jump here.

Upset Alerts

Every matchup ranked by upset likelihood with probability, seed gap, efficiency gap, and specific triggering indicators. Filter by round.

Team Data

Sortable, searchable table of all 68 teams with every stat including Last 10 AdjEM, state, and record. Edit individual stats or paste bulk data. Export picks to clipboard.

Leaderboard

Merged view of human submissions (synced via Firebase) and local sim brackets. Search, filter by champion, paginate through results. Enter game results (admin) to trigger scoring. View any bracket without losing your own.
